Advanced Class

Description

Our Advanced class is tailored for experienced yoga practitioners who are ready to push their limits and explore the depths of their practice. This class incorporates challenging poses and sequences, including inversions and arm balances, designed to test your strength, flexibility, and mental focus. You will experience a vigorous flow that will elevate your heart rate and build endurance.

Expect to sweat and be challenged both physically and mentally. Our expert instructors will guide you through advanced techniques, helping you refine your alignment and deepen your understanding of yoga. This class is perfect for those who are dedicated to their practice and eager to achieve new milestones on their yoga journey.
