Beginner Class

Description

This class is perfect for individuals who are new to yoga and looking to start their journey towards better health and well-being. We will focus on basic poses and techniques, aiming to relieve lower back pain and improve overall flexibility and balance. Each session is designed to be gentle and accessible, providing a solid foundation in yoga practice.

Join us in this calming and supportive environment where you can learn at your own pace. Our instructors are experienced and patient, ready to guide you through each pose with detailed instructions and modifications as needed. Whether you're looking to increase your physical fitness, reduce stress, or just explore something new, this class is the perfect place to start.
