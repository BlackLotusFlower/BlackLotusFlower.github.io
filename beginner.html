Beginner Yoga Class

Welcome to our Beginner Yoga Class! This class is designed for all levels, especially for those just getting into yoga and trying to relieve lower back pain. Our sessions focus on basic postures, breathing techniques, and gentle stretches to help you build a solid foundation in yoga. Our experienced instructors will guide you through each pose with detailed explanations and modifications to ensure you feel comfortable and confident.

You will learn how to align your body properly, improve your flexibility, and develop a greater awareness of your breath and body. This class is perfect for anyone looking to ease tension, reduce stress, and improve overall well-being. Join us and start your yoga journey with a supportive and welcoming community.
