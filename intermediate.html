Intermediate Class

Description

Our Intermediate class is designed for those who have a foundational understanding of yoga and are looking to deepen their practice. This class will challenge you with more complex poses and sequences, helping to build strength, flexibility, and endurance. You will learn to connect your breath with movement, enhancing your overall yoga experience.

Expect a dynamic flow that will push your boundaries and take you out of your comfort zone. Our skilled instructors will provide guidance and support, ensuring that you can safely and effectively progress in your practice. This class is ideal for those who are ready to explore more advanced techniques and take their yoga journey to the next level.
